Utilization Office, NASA, Code KT, Washington, D.C. 20546.\n\nImproved Orthopedic Arm Joint\nAn improved orthopedic (orthotic) arm joint permits smoother and easier movement of a disabled\n\xe2\x80\xa2arm and is smaller and lighter than previous models.\nAll components, including the potentiometer which\ncan be adjusted externally, are mounted within the\ndevice. The new joint incorporates a friction clutch\nfor protection of the gears and motors and can be\nlargely assembled from readily available hardware.\nThe joint is interchangeable and may be used on\neither arm at the shoulder or at the elbow. Its\nmanufacturing cost is estimated to be equal to or\nless than that of existing models.\n\nNote:\nNo additional documentation is available.
