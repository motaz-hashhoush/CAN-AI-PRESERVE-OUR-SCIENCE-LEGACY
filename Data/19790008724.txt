Vulnerability of the ground segment is not\ngreatly different from that of other large industrial complexes, but\nits importance suggests that strong protective measures would be pro-\n\nvided for it.\nThe paper concludes with an outline of some of the key issues\ninvolved, and a number of recommendations \xe2\x82\xacor further study, including\nsome areas for longer term efforts.\n\nV\n\nACKN 9TKED\n\nNT\n\nThe a u t h o r i s d e e p l y i n d e b t e d t o D r .
