HPGP 22 N thruster was designed and built to substantiate\nthe thruster design, build process, and testing to the NASA GSFC PACE\nmission requirements under the auspices of the international IA.\n\xe2\x80\xa2 This test program was developed to comprehensively test the thruster, the\ntechnology, and foremost to increase the HPGP 22 N TRL level.\n\xe2\x80\x93 HPGP 22 N EQM was tested to environmental qualification levels prior to\nhot fire performance testing to represent the relevant end-to-end\nenvironment (launch to on-orbit operation).\n\xe2\x80\xa2 Prior to the discontinuation of the hot firing tests, the HPGP 22 N EQM had\nsuccessfully met all hot fire performance requirements over a wide range of\nsingle, continuous, and pulse mode firings over a feed pressure range\n\xe2\x80\xa2 This work advances the test, analytical, and risk reduction activities for\ncandidate green propellant LMP-103S and HPGP technology.\n\nGODDARD SPACE FLIGHT
