DISSEMINATION (1/2)\nFormat\n\xe2\x80\xa2 originally in flat binary, sometimes encoded\n\xe2\x80\xa2 innovation: add an extra row loaded with metadata\n\xe2\x80\xa2 standards have evolved\n\xe2\x80\xa2 HDF flavors, NetCDF flavors, tending toward interoperability\n\xe2\x80\xa2 still a work in progress, particularly in dealing with libraries for different versions\n\xe2\x80\xa2 data centers are adding web services, format converters, subsetting by parameter\nand location, value-added products (shape file averages, accumulations)\nTime/space interval driven by data availability\n\xe2\x80\xa2 GPCP V1 was 2.5\xc2\xb0/monthly, then introduced 1\xc2\xb0/daily (for a shorter period)\n\xe2\x80\xa2 TMPA V4 was 1\xc2\xb0/monthly and 1\xc2\xb0/daily, then went to 0.25\xc2\xb0/monthly and 0.25\xc2\xb0/3hourly\n\xe2\x80\xa2 IMERG is 0.1\xc2\xb0/monthly and 0.1\xc2\xb0/half-hourly\n\nErrors are higher for finer-scale estimates\n\nHuffman et al. 4\n\n3. DISSEMINATION (2/2)\nMultiple runs accommodate different user requirements for latency and accuracy\n\xe2\x80\xa2 TMPA backed into handing out near-real-time (~8 hours old) by user request\n\xe2\x80\xa2 tapped into a strong demand for current data\n\xe2\x80\xa2 lesson learned: users need a long, consistently processed record for each\ndataset to support calibration of applications\n- the entire record of all variants has to be upgraded in a version shift\n- \xe2\x80\x9creal time\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x89\xa0 \xe2\x80\x9cdisposable\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\xa6 this is a paradigm shift for archive operators\n\xe2\x80\xa2 IMERG has 3 \xe2\x80\x9cRuns\xe2\x80\x9d to support a wider variety of use cases\n\xe2\x80\xa2 \xe2\x80\x9cEarly\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x93 4 hr (flash flooding)\n\xe2\x80\xa2 \xe2\x80\x9cLate\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x93 12 hr (crop forecasting)\n\xe2\x80\xa2 \xe2\x80\x9cFinal\xe2\x80\x9d \xe2\x80\x93 3 months (research)\n\xe2\x80\xa2 waiting longer includes more input data \xe2\x80\x93 presumably more accuracy\n\xe2\x80\xa2 lesson learned: users get confused when confronted with multiple products\n- this is also true across data providers\n- data providers have to do a better job at \xe2\x80\x9csuitability for use\xe2\x80\x9d information\n\nHuffman et al. 5\n\n4.
