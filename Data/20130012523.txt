ABSTRACT\n\nIn response to concerns about risks from lead-free induced faults to high reliability products, NASA has initiated a multi-year\nproject to provide manufacturers and users with data to clarify the risks of lead-free materials in their products. The project will\nalso be of interest to component manufacturers supplying to high reliability markets.\nThe project was launched in November 2006. The primary technical objective of the project is to undertake comprehensive testing\nto generate information on failure modes/criteria to better understand the reliability of:\n- Packages (e.g., TSOP, BGA, PDIP) assembled and reworked with solder interconnects consisting of lead-free alloys\n- Packages (e.g., TSOP, BGA, PDIP) assembled and reworked with solder interconnects consisting of mixed alloys, lead\ncomponent finish/lead-free solder and lead-free component fmish/SnPb solder\n15.
