qIR\n\xef\x81\xac\n\nand\n\nqemitted = \xce\xb5IR \xcf\x83Ts4\n\nSetting these equal and solving for Ts yields\n\nTs = { [qs (\xce\xb1s /\xce\xb5IR) + qIR ] / \xcf\x83 } 1/4\nwhere qs = solar-spectrum
