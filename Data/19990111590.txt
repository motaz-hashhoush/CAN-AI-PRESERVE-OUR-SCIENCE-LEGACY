Below the Mixed Layer\nBelow the ocean mixed layer, the external wind-generated shear i_ too small to\ngenerateturbulent mixing and yet, even in regions where both the temperature and the\nsalinity gradients are stably stratified, it is usual to assume"background diffusivities" for\nviscosity, heat and salt diffusivity which are believed to be caused by internal wave\nbreaking (Large et al., 1997).
