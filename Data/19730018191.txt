Abstract\nThis Register is comprised of a list of approximately 150 experts from\napproximately 60 organizations who have published results of theoretical and/or\nexperimental research related to six problem areas in the mechanics of structural\nfailure. Each author included is listed by organizational affiliation, address\nand principal field of expertise.\nThe initial criteria for the selection of names for the register are recent\ncontributions to the literature, participation in or support of relevant research\nprograms, and referral by peers.\nThe purpose of the register is to present, in easy reference form, sources for\ndependable information regarding failure modes and mechanisms -of aerospace\nstructures. The Register Includes two indexes: an alphabetical listing of the\nexperts and an alphabetical listing of the organizations with whom they are\naffiliated.\n\n17. There are also included areas of\nexpertise which may be of use in the near future. In most cases the area\nof expertise was described by the expert accounting for variations in\nwording.\nThe purpose of this publication is to present, in easy reference form,\nsources for dependable information regarding failure modes and mechanisms\nof aerospace structures. The experts, who are points of contact, could be\nqueried by researchers in similar fields of research and development work\nfor unpublished information and more of the details which have not appeared\nin published reports, journal articles, or society presentations. In\naddition, further inquiries could be made regarding active ongoing projects\nwhere the published results may not appear for several months. No attempt\nwas made to ascertain that persons listed are actually available for\nextensive consultation. This is left to negotiation between the parties\ninvolved.\nThe organizational affiliation reflects the latest reference document\nwhich was reviewed and is not necessarily current.\nSelection for inclusion was based on the independent judgments of an author\'s\npeers as well as the number of his appropriate documents uncovered by a\nstaff of technical reviewers. This is recognized as being an incomplete\nlisting and represents only an initial installment. Nevertheless, we hope\nthat it will contribute as a guide to those who seek related information.\nCandidates for a revised register are encouraged.\nThe authors wish to acknowledge the interest and assistance of the following\nindividuals in the compilation of this Register: James E. Campbell, Battelle\nMemorial Institute; James C. Freche, NASA Lewis Research Center; and\nGeorge C.
