Then the following \t\n\ni\nIT\n\nparameters were varied:\n\nNumber of Missions - The number of missions that a payload would be flown was\n\nanticipated to have a significant effect on test plan selection. The previous studies\n\xc2\xb5\t\n\nwere for a 15 mission facility type payload. As the number of missions is reduced,\nit was anticipated that it could be cost effective to lower the vibroacoustic\n"\t\n\nreliability resulting in reduced test levels and possibly select an alternate test\nplan. To evaluate this effect, three payload configurations from the previous\nstudies were chosen with the number of missions reduced to 1 and 8.\n\n`y\t\n\nComponent Failure Probability - One-of the most difficult parameters to quantify is\n\nthe component failure. probability.
