Easy to search using Google\nor its internal search.\n\n\xe2\x80\xa2\n\nEasy to connect with - many applications directly\ndownload spectra and data for use in real time.\n\n\xe2\x80\xa2\n\nCustom programmed for this data. Expensive to\nmodify what it can store.\n\n\xe2\x80\xa2\n\nMany scientists have requested a \xe2\x80\x9ccopy\xe2\x80\x9d to store\ntheir data.\n\n\xe2\x80\xa2\n\nCan we abstract it to store and plot nearly any data?\n\nData Management System\n\xe2\x80\xa2\n\nBob Downs (University of Arizona) and\nDavid Blake (NASA Ames) conceived the\nidea of abstracting the RRUFF database so\nthat other researchers could organize and\nshare their research data.\n\n\xe2\x80\xa2\n\nInspired by Wordpress - shown that simple\ncontent publication tools result in an\nexplosion of new material.\n\n\xe2\x80\xa2\n\nWikipedia and the WikiMedia Foundation our goal is to create the software and\nprovide it free so that anyone can create a\ndatabase and put it online.\n\n\xe2\x80\xa2\n\nTailored for publishing and archiving\nresearch data.\n\nProject Plan\n\xe2\x80\xa2\n\nProject broken into two phases:\n\xe2\x80\xa2\n\nPhase 1: Use-case pilot\n\xe2\x80\xa2\n\n\xe2\x80\xa2\n\xe2\x80\xa2\n\nDetermine if we can make a tool that \xef\xac\x81ts into a researcher\xe2\x80\x99s work\xef\xac\x82ow rather than an\nadditional task that comes when the work is complete.\nCurrently in progress\n\nPhase 2: Semantic web integration/General release\n\xe2\x80\xa2\n\nDevelop tools to map data to ontologies, allow ontology-based data creation, RDFa, etc.\n\n\xe2\x80\xa2\n\nCitation and snapshot-based archiving system.\n\n\xe2\x80\xa2\n\nFocus of Q2-Q4 2016\n\nMarket and Objective\n\xe2\x80\xa2\n\nTarget the long tail of research data that fall outside of larger initiatives.\n\xe2\x80\xa2\n\nAccessible to individual researchers or small teams.\n\xe2\x80\xa2\n\nRuns on a standard LAMP (Linux, Apache, MySQL, PHP) server.\n\n\xe2\x80\xa2\n\nHosted service or host themselves to provide options when there are ownership issues.\n\n\xe2\x80\xa2\n\nDoesn\xe2\x80\x99t require a specialist or signi\xef\xac\x81cant training to run.\n\n\xe2\x80\xa2\n\nDetermine what types of data we would be able to support with a simple, web-based tool (spectra, sample collection data,\nsite data, etc.)\n\n\xe2\x80\xa2\n\nHelp researchers organize their data from collection through analysis and \xef\xac\x81nally to preservation.\n\n\xe2\x80\xa2\n\nProvide a complete audit trail and provenance chain: raw data through to presented analysis.\n\n\xe2\x80\xa2\n\nFacilitate sharing and re-use by easing mapping to metadata standards, ontologies and providing semantic searches\nacross databases. (Phase 2)\n\n\xe2\x80\xa2\n\nArchiving of data snapshots/releases within the system and publishing of snapshots to third party repositories.
