Canadian deme4stie satellite eemmunieations system wil use satellites\nlocated in synchronous, circular, equatorial orbits, as relay stations for a\n:network of earth stations. The system will provide telephone message traffic,\nand television and radio program distribution.\nThe mission dbjective is to place a communications satellite in a geostationary orbit at a longitude of approximately 114 degrees west giving a desired\ncoverage region which will include most of Canada.
