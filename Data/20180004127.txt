monitoring Constellation safety\nof the Earth Sciences Constellation (ESC) missions and is a\ncentral source of data sharing and operational planning.\n\xe2\x80\x93 Primary tool for monitoring the Constellation configurations.\n\xe2\x80\x93 Enables information exchange among/between domestic and\ninternational partner ESC missions, including access to nominal\npredicted mission ephemerides.\n\xe2\x80\x93 Transfer critical product data between the Mission Operation Centers\n(MOCs), Conjunction Assessment Risk Analysis (CARA), and other\nauthorized mission users.\n\xe2\x80\x93 Mission Analysis tools and automated health and safety monitoring.\n\xe2\x80\xa2 Automated constellation safety warning notifications.\n\xe2\x80\xa2 Graphical visualization of orbital data.\n\n\xe2\x80\xa2 The latest release, CCS 2018.1, was deployed to operations on\n11 April 2018.\n5\n\nMission Operations Working Group\nJune 12 \xe2\x80\x93 14, 2018\n\nCCS 2018.1\nReleased: 11
