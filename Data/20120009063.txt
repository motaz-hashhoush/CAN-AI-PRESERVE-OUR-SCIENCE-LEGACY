b'OPPORTUNITIES FOR COOPERATION:\nTHEMIS AND RBSP\n\nD. G. Sibeck\nTHEMIS/ARTEMIS Project Scientist\nRBSP Mission Scientist\n\nExecutive Summary. This document outlines potential joint scientific studies involving the\nTHEMIS and RBSP missions as a function of mission phase from the commissioning of RBSP\nin August 2012 through the end of its prime mission in the summer of 2014. It describes\nconsensus recommendations for interspace craft separation, fast survey and burst mode strategies\nfor THEMIS that will enable the two missions to achieve and exceed their common objectives.\nAn appendix describes the need for careful intercalibration of THEMIS and RBSP particle\nobservations.\n\n'
