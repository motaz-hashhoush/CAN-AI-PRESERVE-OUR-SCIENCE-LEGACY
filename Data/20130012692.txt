STAGE INTEGRATION - OVERALL TEAM DESIGN PRINCIPLE\nDETERMINATOR\n\xe2\x80\xa2 Flight hardware and ground support systems grouped by "stage" to align\nLaunch Team structure with processing team structure, leveraging that\nexpertise in the launch environment.\n- Launch Vehicle stage\n- Space Craft stage\n- Ground Support Systems stage\n\nEach stage has it\'s own fully-functional test team\n- Test management\n- Engineering\n- Safety & Mission Assurance\n\nProblem identification and resolution is achieved within a stage (if possible).\nSystem/Discipline Engineering, Integration Engineering, Test Conductor,\nwith S&MA insight, resolve issues with their stage test team.\n\xe2\x80\xa2 Problems whose characteristics indicate effects may affect other stages\n(i.e., Launch Vehicle issue affects Space Craft readiness) are integrated at\noverall Launch Integration level\n- Launch Test Director\n- Launch Integration Engineer\n- Mission Assurance Manager\n\nwhile utilizing system/discipline engineering expertise from the stages for\nproblem resolution.\n\nCxP Launch Team Major Design Features\n2. ONE SINGLE LAUNCH TEAM\n\xe2\x80\xa2 The Launch Team will be comprised of a single team with GO / NO\nGO authority, with subordinate (and functionally aligned) supporting\nteam members\n- Prime Launch Team (PLT) - implementation of documented CxP\nRequirements, GO / NO GO authority. Located in KSC LCC FR I\n"Front Room"\n- Support Launch Team (SLT) - system/functional experts are an\navailable resource to PLT members if requested. Located in KSC LCC\nFRI "Back Room"\n- Extended Support Team - Design Center, Original Equipment\nManufacturers Team - design expertise available to SLT I PLT if\nrequested. Located external to FR I.\n\n\xe2\x80\xa2 All team members with GO I NO GO authority will be located in the\nKSC, LCC Firing Room I, Front Room (less off-site required\nfunctions like MILA, Range, MOD, etc.)\nE*]\n\nCxP Launch Team Major Design Features\n3. GO I NO GO PHILOSOPHY\n\xe2\x80\xa2 GO / NO GO Authority will be granted to only those\npositions/persons with the responsibility and required expertise to\ncommit their system/stage to launch\n- Significantly fewer GO / NO GO polls than Shuttle\n- No "Feel-Good" GO / NO GO polls\n- Prime Launch Team members can question specific Support Launch\nTeam members if a concern exists, but NO formal poll. Likewise for the\nExtended Support Team members.\n\n\xe2\x80\xa2 Expanding polls beyond those responsible for, and with the\nAuthority to give the GO / NO GO dilutes meaning of the poll.\n- "Group Think"\n- "Feel Good"\n\n* Keep it lean. Keep it meaningful *\n\nCxP Launch Team Major Design Features\n4.
