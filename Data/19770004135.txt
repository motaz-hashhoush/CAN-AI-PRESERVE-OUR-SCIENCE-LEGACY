This information will be provided\nin the form of rendezvous sensitivities to predictor errors. In order\nto establish predictor specifications which are independent of actual\npredictor techniques, the approach employs a relatively simple predictor\nas a "truth model" to establish a reference trajectory and a precisely\ndefined error is added to the "truth model" to determine the effects\nof the error on the trajectory. This technique assumes that the differences\nbetween the actual trajectory and the predictor used onboard will result\nin the same sensitivities.\n\nThe OFT trajectory was targeted with and without errors.
