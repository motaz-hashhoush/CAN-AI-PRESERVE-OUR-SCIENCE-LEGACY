conditions: 10 cfm, 20 minute half cycles (shorter if running less than 24 hours/day)\n\xe2\x80\x93 Chosen in order to allow operation for longer time periods while remaining under the noise, air loss\nand water loss constraints (~976 hrs total test time)\n\n\xef\x83\x98\n\nMode B - High blower speed test:\n\xe2\x80\x93 Projected conditions: 26 cfm (or max), 6.5 minute half cycles\n\xe2\x80\x93 Must be performed in shorter time increments in order to remain under the noise, air loss and water\nloss constraints (~24 hrs total test time)\n\nExperiment Nominal Test
