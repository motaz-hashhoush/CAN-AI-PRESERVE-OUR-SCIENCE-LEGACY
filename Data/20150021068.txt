molecular radius, TML, ...)\nload component data (temperature, total mass, cleanliness level, ...)\ninitialize surface data structures\nfor each timestep:\n%particle push\nfor each particle:\nif surface impact:\ncompute residence time\nif greater than time step:\ndeposit to surface layer\nelse:\nre-emit following cosine law\n%outgassing model\nfor each component:\ncompute mass outgassing mass loss\nadd to surface layer\n%desorption\nfor each surface element:\ncompute desorption loss\ninject particles with cosine distribution\ndeplete surface layer\n19\n\nContamination Analysis Automatically computes delta deltas and TGA.\nRGA Extractor: Java program for loading multiple SRS RGA scan log files and\nexporting pressure vs. time data over all of them. Partial pressures can be\ncomputed over a range of values.\nContamination Transport Simulation Program: C++ code for modeling\ntransport of dust particulates and molecules. Particulates can be sampled\nfrom IEST-STD-1246 distribution and can be accelerated by aerodynamic\nforces. Detailed surface model supporting outgassing and desorption is\nused instead of sticking coefficients for free molecular flow. Allows to\nresolve temporal effects such as warming up of surfaces.\n\nQuestions?\nContact: lubos.brieda@particleincell.com\n\n27\n\n'
