primary sequence\nof the genomic DNA.\n\nWe know that changes in these decorations can\nalter gene expression, and further, that plants\nuse this approach to physiologically adjust to\nadverse environments on Earth.\nFor the APEX-04 spaceflight experiment we\nwill re-sequence the genomes to see how\nchanges in DNA-methylation contribute to the\nresponse.\nThese experiments will provide a key piece of\nthe puzzle of how plants adjust their\nphysiology to meet the needs of growing in a\nplace outside their evolutionary experience.\nAnd the more complete our understanding, the\nmore success we will have in future missions as\nwe take plants with us off planet.\n3\n\nAPEX-04 Science Overview\n\xe2\x80\xa2 APEX-04 will specifically address the role of epigenetic DNA\nmethylation in
