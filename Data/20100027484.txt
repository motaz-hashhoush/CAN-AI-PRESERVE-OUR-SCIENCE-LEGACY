operational challenges associated with enabling\nroutine UAS access to the NAS\nThis goal will be accomplished through a two-phased approach of system-level integration of key\nconcepts, technologies and/or procedures, and demonstrations of integrated capabilities in an\noperationally relevant environment. Technical objectives include:\nPHASE 1\n\xe2\x80\xa2 Validating the key technical areas identified by this project. System-level analyses, a State of the\nArt Analysis (SOAA), and a ConOps will identify the challenges and barriers preventing routine\nUAS access to the NAS.\n\xe2\x80\xa2 Developing a national roadmap and gap analysis identifying specific deliverables in the area of\noperations, procedures, and technologies that will impact future policy decisions.\nPHASE 2\n\xe2\x80\xa2 Provide regulators with a methodology for developing airworthiness requirements for UAS and\ndata to support development of certifications standards and regulatory guidance.\n\xe2\x80\xa2 Provide systems-level integrated testing of concepts and/or capabilities that address barriers to\nroutine access to the NAS. Through simulation and flight testing, address issues including\nseparation assurance, communications requirements, and Pilot Aircraft Interfaces (PAIs) in\noperationally relevant environments.\n7\n\nProject Scope\n\xe2\x80\xa2 Demonstrate solutions in four specific technology disciplines, which will\naddress operational and/or safety issues related UAS access to
